RDBMS Features Demonstrated

This School ERP system showcases every aspect of SimpleSQLDB's capabilities through real-world scenarios:

Foreign Keys: Enrollments → Users, Courses
INNER JOIN: Students with their enrolled courses
GROUP BY & HAVING: Top performers by avg grade
Aggregates: SUM, AVG, COUNT for analytics
B-Tree Indexing: Fast lookups on user_id, course_id
Bulk Operations: Import 50+ students at once
Complex WHERE: Multi-condition filtering
Real-time Logs: Every SQL operation tracked
